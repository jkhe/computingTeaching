Who runs the kitchen?

Who coordinates every order and every worker?

The Head Chef = Operating System (OS)

The OS manages all hardware and software. It decides what happens, when, and in what order. Examples: Chrome OS, Windows, iOS, Android.

🔥
Who does the actual cooking?

Who carries out the orders and does the hard work?

The Chefs = CPU (Processor)

The CPU follows every instruction from the OS. It processes everything — calculations, graphics, inputs. Very fast, but cannot decide anything on its own.

🔪
Where is food prepared right now?

What gets wiped clean when the kitchen closes?

The Countertop = RAM (Memory)

RAM holds whatever the computer is working on right now. It is fast but temporary — everything disappears when the power goes off.

🥫
Where are the recipes kept?

What stores everything even when the kitchen is shut?

The Pantry = Storage (SSD / HDD)

Storage saves files, apps and the OS itself permanently. Even with no power, everything stays. It is slower than RAM but holds far more.
